About Sharpen Sweet

Passionate about perfect edges since 2010

Founded by master sharpener Tony Ferlazzo, Sharpen Sweet began with a simple mission: to bring professional-level knife sharpening services to home cooks and professional chefs alike.

With over 5 years of experience in the sharpening industry, Tony recognized that even the best knives lose their edge over time, and most people don't have the skills or equipment to properly restore them.

Today, our team of skilled technicians sharpen thousands of knives each year, combining traditional techniques with modern precision equipment to deliver exceptional results every time.

We're passionate about educating our customers on proper knife care and maintenance, and we take pride in helping you rediscover the joy of cooking with truly sharp knives.
