Our Sharpening Process

Meticulous care from start to finish

Inspection & Assessment

We carefully inspect your knives for chips, cracks, and assess the current edge condition.

Initial Grinding

Using precision equipment, we reshape the edge to remove damage and restore proper geometry.

Progressive Refining

We use increasingly fine grits to remove scratches and create a smooth, sharp edge.

Hand Finishing

Final polishing by hand using traditional water stones for the ultimate sharp edge.

Testing & Quality Control

Every knife is tested for sharpness and performance before being returned to you.
